Getting started

01

Download & install

Grab Ledgerly.Setup.0.1.0.exe and double-click it. Installation finishes in seconds and puts Ledgerly on your desktop and Start Menu.

02

Click past the Windows warning

The first time you run the installer, Windows SmartScreen shows "Windows protected your PC." This is normal for new independent apps — click More info, then Run anyway.

03

Create your account

Open Ledgerly and sign up with email & password or Google. Enter your business name and primary currency — that creates your business and makes you its owner.

04

Joining someone else's business?

If a business owner gave you an invite code, enter it on the sign-up form (or, if you already have an account, click the business name in the top-left of the app and use "Join another business"). You'll join their business with the role they assigned.

05

Make it yours

In Settings → Business, upload your logo — it will appear on every PDF you export. Then head to Settings → Team to generate invite codes for your teammates.

First login of the day can take up to a minute while the server wakes up. After that, everything is fast. If a login seems stuck, give it a moment and try once more.
AI Insights

Unlock unlimited AI analysis

AI Insights works out of the box on a shared free quota of 10 messages per day per business. For unlimited use, connect your own free Google Gemini API key — it takes about a minute and doesn't require a credit card.

01

Open Google AI Studio

Go to aistudio.google.com/apikey and sign in with any Google account.

02

Create an API key

Click "Create API key". Google generates a long key instantly — click the copy button next to it. (Free, no payment method needed.)

03

Paste it into Ledgerly

In the app, open Settings → Business → AI Insights, paste your key, and hit Save. (Only business owners and admins can do this.)

04

Chat away

That's it — your whole team now has unlimited AI Insights, covered by your key's own generous free tier. Usage is tied to your Google account, and your key is never shown to anyone after saving.

Tip: ask things like "Where am I spending the most, and what can I cut?" or "How is my cash flow trending month over month?" — the AI answers using your real, current books.
Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Is Ledgerly free?

Yes. The app is free to download and use. AI Insights includes a free shared quota, and you can connect your own free Gemini key for unlimited use.

Where is my data stored?

Your data is stored securely in a cloud database and synced through an encrypted (HTTPS) connection — so your books are the same on any computer you sign in from, and nothing is lost if your PC dies.

What are the roles, and what can each one do?

Owner — created the business; full control. Admin — everything the owner can do day-to-day: manage the team, payroll, business settings. Staff — can work with transactions, invoices, and reports, but cannot see or run payroll or manage the team.

Can I belong to more than one business?

Yes. Redeem an invite code from another business (click the business name in the top-left of the app → "Join another business") and switch between businesses anytime from that same menu.

Why does Windows warn me when installing?

Ledgerly isn't yet code-signed with a paid certificate, so Windows SmartScreen is cautious with it. The installer is safe — click More info → Run anyway.

Does it work offline?

No — Ledgerly needs an internet connection, since your data lives in the cloud and syncs in real time.

How do currencies work?

Your business has a primary currency used for dashboards and reports, and each record keeps the currency it was created in. The dashboard also shows live exchange rates for reference. If you switch your primary currency, Settings offers a one-click relabel of existing records.
